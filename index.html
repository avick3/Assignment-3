About Me

Hello there! My name is Abby Vick, and I am a second-year student in the GBDA Program at the University of Waterloo. My journey into design began over 10 years ago when I first used an iPad with my cousin and explored VideoStar. It wasn’t until grade 10 when I took Communications Technology that I realized I could turn my passion for creating into a career. My time at UW has been amazing so far, and I’m excited to continue learning and growing as a designer and as a person.
